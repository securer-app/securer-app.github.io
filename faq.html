Q: How is my data ciphered?

AES encryption is used. Your password is passed trhough a KDF called BCrypt.

Q: Where are my data saved?

It's is saved in JSON format in the private preferences of your device or if you exported it you can save it to Google drive or a file.

Q: Does securer remebers my securer master pass?

Nope. It would be easy to crack your passwords if so.

Q: What do i do if i lost securer pass?

Securer can't do anything as it don't store the master pass. Sorry.
